Crafted by Nature. Perfected by allinwood.

ALLINWOOD’s journey began in 2004, deep within Europe’s forests, where our craft took root. Starting with raw timber, we honed our knowledge at every stage — from sourcing and milling to construction and finishing. This hands-on experience became our signature: an unmatched understanding of wood behavior, quality, and timeless design.

For over two decades, we’ve transformed nature’s raw beauty into precision-crafted surfaces. Specializing in engineered European White Oak flooring and wooden panels, our products are made in Europe and finished with natural, VOC-free oils — offering both sustainability and sophistication.

In 2024, we took our commitment to innovation even further by developing a new line of engineered thermally modified wood. This technology enhances the wood’s natural durability, dimensional stability, and resistance to moisture and insects — without the use of chemicals. It opens the door to our next chapter: premium hardwood siding and decking, designed to perform beautifully in outdoor environments while maintaining the refined look that defines ALLINWOOD.

From boutique hotels to luxury homes, our products elevate every interior — and now, exteriors — with quiet elegance and enduring performance. We don’t just supply hardwood — we embody it. Every plank reflects our legacy of craftsmanship and commitment to excellence.

Welcome to the collection that reflects your story

We craft more than just flooring — we shape the atmosphere of your home. Each collection is a curated blend of character, innovation, and timeless appeal. Our materials speak for themselves, rich in texture and steeped in natural elegance.
